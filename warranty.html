Warranty Information

Warranty Details

All products come with a standard warranty of 1 year from the date of purchase.

For any issues, please contact our customer service within the warranty period.

How to Claim Warranty

  1. Keep your purchase receipt as proof of purchase.
  2. Contact our customer service via phone or email.
  3. Provide details of the issue and your product information.
  4. Follow the instructions provided by our support team.
